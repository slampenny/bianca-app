## INSTRUCTIONS FOR COMPLETING THIS FORM

**For Hiring Managers**:
1. Complete Sections 1-6

3. Email to security@myphonefriend.com
4. Expect approval within 1-2 business days

1. Review for minimum necessary compliance

3. Check background check (if required)
4. Approve/deny/modify in Section 8
5. Forward to IT for implementation (if approved)
